We are an experiential events agency based in Venice and the Veneto region. We work with companies, institutions, travel agencies and private clients who require the highest standards of execution, professional localisation and access to venues not available through ordinary channels.

Our value lies not in creativity for its own sake, but in the ability to translate a briefing into an event that works: on time, on budget, with the quality perceived by guests and aligned with the client's objectives.

We manage the entire supply chain. From the initial briefing call to the post-event report.
