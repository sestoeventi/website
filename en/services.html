Services

A complete operational partner. Not just a service provider.

The difference between an agency and an operational partner is accountability. We don't hand off problems to vendors or push decisions back to clients. We own the entire supply chain and deliver results.

Our Services

Wedding Planning

From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations. Venue selection, catering coordination, logistics management and day-of execution. We handle the complexity so you can focus on the moment.

Gala Dinners & Shows

High-impact evening events with entertainment, technical production and multi-course catering. Corporate galas, charity fundraisers, private celebrations in extraordinary venues.

Cultural Events

Exhibitions, performances, conferences and symposiums. We manage venue logistics, technical requirements, catering and attendee experience.

Incentive & Team Building

Multi-day programs designed to motivate teams and strengthen company culture. Accommodation, activities, dining and logistics across the Veneto region.

Conventions & Product Launches

Strategic corporate events with technical infrastructure, registration management, catering and entertainment. Designed to maximize engagement and brand impact.

Food & Wine Experiences

Curated gastronomic experiences showcasing Veneto's culinary heritage. Farm visits, winery tours, cooking demonstrations and multi-course tastings.

Location Scouting

Access to exclusive, non-listed venues across Venice and the Veneto. From historic palaces to private estates. Shortlists, site visits and contract negotiation included.

DMC Venice

Destination management for groups. Logistical support, accommodation coordination, activities and ground transportation across the Venetian region.

How We Work

1

Briefing

We listen to your vision, objectives, guest experience goals and budget. No structured form required—just a conversation about what matters to you.

2

Proposal

We develop a detailed proposal with venue options, timeline, budget breakdown and logistics plan. Clear scope, transparent costs, realistic timelines.

3

Validation

Site visits, vendor meetings, contract review. We validate every element of the plan with you before moving forward. You approve the direction, not just the concept.

4

Production

We coordinate the entire supply chain. Regular updates, contingency management, real-time problem-solving. One point of contact throughout.

5

Closing

Post-event reporting with attendance data, budget reconciliation, feedback analysis and lessons learned. Documentation for your records and strategic planning.
