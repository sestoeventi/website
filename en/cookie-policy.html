Information on the use of cookies on sestoeventi.it, pursuant to Art. 122 of Italian Legislative Decree 196/2003 and the guidelines of the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante).

Last updated: March 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites visited by the user send to their device (computer, tablet, smartphone), where they are stored and then retransmitted to the same websites on the next visit. Cookies allow the site to recognise the user's device and remember certain information about their preferences or past actions.

Cookies may be set directly by the site being visited (first-party cookies) or by third-party websites or web services (third-party cookies).

2. Cookies used by this website

The website sestoeventi.it uses only strictly necessary technical cookies required for the site to function. No profiling cookies or third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes are used.

3. How to manage or disable cookies

Users can manage, disable or delete cookies through their browser settings. Please note that disabling technical cookies may impair the correct functioning of certain parts of the site (e.g. submission of the contact form).
