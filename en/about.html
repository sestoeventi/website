About Us

We organise complex events across the Veneto region. With deep specialisation in Venice, Treviso and the lagoon territory.

We operate across the entire Veneto region, with deep expertise in Venice, Treviso and the surrounding lagoon territory. In Venice, in particular, operating is unlike anywhere else. Water access instead of road access. Tide schedules instead of traffic patterns. Municipal restrictions on noise, lighting and guest capacity. Compressed timelines for setup and breakdown. The logistics of moving people, equipment and catering through narrow calle and across water requires different thinking.

This complexity is our distinctive competence. We don't avoid it or work around it — we build every event with the territory's character as a feature, not a limitation. From the Palladian villas and wineries of Treviso to the historic palaces of Venice, we have contingency plans for the contingency plans. We coordinate with supply chain partners across the Veneto who understand the demands of every location. We've learned from hundreds of events what works, where the real pressure points are, and how to deliver seamlessly in every setting.

We work with corporate event managers, procurement directors and institutional clients who are used to detailed briefs, KPIs, and clear reporting. You'll have a single point of contact, transparent budgeting, and a post-event report that documents what happened, what worked, and what we learned together. We treat your event as a strategic project, not a transaction.
