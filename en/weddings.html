Weddings & Wedding Planning

The Veneto region as a wedding venue requires a partner who knows it from the inside.

Organising a wedding in Venice cannot be replicated using the standard event management playbook. Water-based logistics, heritage authority restrictions, international guest management, tides, municipal permits for public ceremony spaces: every variable requires specific competencies and relationships built over time with local authorities.

We work both directly with couples and as subcontractors for international wedding planners who need a structured local partner. In both cases, our contribution is measurable: clean coordination, reliable suppliers, no unmanaged variables on the day of the event.

International couples

SESTO's added value

Institutional relationships

Direct relationships with municipal authorities, heritage bodies and permit offices. We navigate the bureaucratic landscape that international planners cannot.

Local network depth

Years of working with vetted suppliers means we know not just what they offer, but how they perform under pressure and in complex logistics.

Lagoon expertise

Understanding Venice's unique environment—tides, water transport, heritage preservation, seasonal variations—is not transferable. We have built this knowledge operationally.

End-to-end responsibility

No gaps in coordination. We take operational ownership from venue selection through post-event follow-up, ensuring consistency across all elements.

International guest experience

Multilingual reception, accommodation coordination, transport logistics and cultural briefing ensure international guests experience Venice professionally, not chaotically.

Risk mitigation

Contingency planning for weather, tides, technical issues and exceptional circumstances. Venice's complexity requires scenario planning that protects your event.

Our approach

01

Discovery & briefing

Detailed conversation to understand your vision, guest profile, budget parameters and timeline. Identification of venue shortlist aligned with requirements.

02

Venue visits & negotiation

Site tours, technical assessments and proposal development. Negotiation of terms and confirmation of availability and permits.

03

Supplier selection

Shortlisting and proposals from catering, floral design, photography, entertainment and additional services. Budget alignment and contracts.

04

Detailed planning

Menu tastings, design previews, logistics planning, guest communication and contingency scenario mapping.

05

Final preparations

Detailed run-of-show documentation, supplier briefings, timeline synchronisation and guest accommodation confirmation.

06

Event day execution

Full operational direction, real-time coordination and invisible management of all logistics and contingencies.
