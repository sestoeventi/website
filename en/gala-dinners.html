Gala Dinners & Shows

A gala dinner is not an elegant meal. It is a communication operation.

A gala dinner has a precise objective: to create a lasting impression on guests. Whether they are top clients, institutional partners or internal stakeholders, the quality of the experience translates directly into the perception of the brand or organisation that produced it.

Our contribution in this type of event is the ability to hold together creative concept, gastronomic quality, evening direction and operational reliability in a complex operational context like Venice.

Who this service is for

Corporate event managers

Responsible for producing high-stakes events for board meetings, client appreciation or internal milestone celebrations.

Communications directors

Managing brand reputation and stakeholder relationships through strategic experiential events.

PR agencies

Producing gala events on behalf of corporate clients requiring full operational and creative management.

What's included in our service

Concept & Art Direction

Definition of the visual theme and colour palette. Suggested dress code guidance for staff. Graphic coherence across invitations, menus, place settings and staging elements to create unified aesthetic impact.

Exclusive Venue

Selection from our Location Scouting portfolio. Each candidate venue is assessed for technical suitability (lighting, acoustics, catering infrastructure) as well as visual and atmospheric qualities.

Signature Menu

Menu developed in collaboration with selected local chefs. Advance technical tasting for approval. Management of specific dietary requirements including allergies, cultural diets, kosher and halal standards on request.

Evening Direction

Minute-by-minute operational plan including speech timing, service choreography, audio and lighting cues, and incident management that remains invisible to guests.

Integrated Entertainment

Music and performances conceived as integral to the concept narrative, not as separate add-ons. Curated from a network of professionals experienced in formal settings and high-level production standards.

Guest Logistics

Welcome coordination, water transfers, cloakroom management, VIP protocols and security coordination. The entire guest-facing service infrastructure that determines perceived quality without visible effort.

SESTO's added value

Concept coherence

We integrate creative direction with operational reality. Concepts that look impressive in presentation decks but fail in execution are corrected before production.

Culinary partnerships

Relationships with Veneto's top chefs means we develop menus that reflect current culinary thinking, not standardised corporate catering templates.

Venue knowledge

We know the technical capabilities of each venue: power distribution, water pressure, acoustic properties. This prevents promises made to clients that cannot be fulfilled.

Operational excellence

Every element is rehearsed. Service staff are briefed. Timing is validated. On event day, guests experience seamless flow while all complexity is managed invisibly behind the scenes.

Venice expertise

Producing formal events in Venice means managing water-based logistics, heritage venue requirements and municipal restrictions. This is not standard knowledge in event management.

Crisis containment

When unexpected issues arise—and they do—our team manages them without disrupting guest experience. This capability is invisible until it is needed.

Our approach

01

Strategic briefing

Understanding the business objective. Who are the key guests? What impression must they have? What is the brand or organisation positioning this event communicates?

02

Concept development

Creative concept proposal aligned with strategic objective. Visual mood boards, preliminary design direction, suggested venues and estimated production scope.

03

Venue & culinary planning

Site visit and technical assessment. Chef collaboration for menu tasting and approval. Timeline and logistics preliminary mapping.

04

Detailed operational planning

Run-of-show development with precise timing. Service choreography planning. Entertainment brief and rehearsal schedule. Contingency scenarios.

05

Production execution

Setup and technical checks. Staff briefing and rehearsal. Final preparations and guest welcome coordination.

06

Event evening direction

Real-time coordination and management. Speech cuing and timing. Service direction. Entertainment coordination. Incident response and adaptation as needed.
