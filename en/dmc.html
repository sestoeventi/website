DMC — Destination Management

Your operational point of reference in Venice and the Veneto.

As a Destination Management Company, we are the local partner that agencies, tour operators and HR departments identify as their operational reference for any initiative taking place in the Venetian and Veneto territory. Our added value compared to a generic supplier lies in our deep knowledge of the destination, our network of qualified partners and our ability to manage the logistical complexity of the lagoon.

Tour operators

International travel companies offering Venice and Veneto packages requiring reliable local operational management.

SESTO's added value

Lagoon expertise

Understanding Venice's water-based logistics, tidal variations, heritage restrictions and environmental complexity is not standard DMC knowledge. We have built this through years of on-ground operation.

Supplier relationships

Direct partnerships with accommodation providers, restaurants, guides and activity operators. This means guaranteed quality, reliable availability and commercial terms optimised for agency partners.

Operational continuity

We maintain the same team across multiple bookings. Groups benefit from consistent, personalised service rather than generic vendor relationships.

Crisis management

Venice presents unique operational challenges: seasonal flooding, weather disruption, heritage site closures. We manage these contingencies professionally and invisibly.

Quality assurance

Pre-visit verification of all venues, transport and activity providers. Post-event feedback and continuous quality monitoring ensure standards remain consistent.

Commercial efficiency

Standard industry commission structure with transparent, negotiated terms. Volume discounts and special arrangements available for regular partners.

Our DMC process

01

Initial briefing

Group size, arrival/departure dates, budget parameters, special requirements and group profile. Destination experience and activity preferences noted.

02

Proposal development

Customised itinerary options with accommodation, transport, activities and catering. Multiple options at different price points provided for selection.

03

Booking and confirmation

Contract execution with agreed itinerary, rates and terms. Deposit and payment schedule established. Supplier confirmations secured.

04

Detailed planning

Accommodation check-in logistics, transfer schedules, activity briefings and special arrangement coordination (accessibility, dietary requirements, VIP protocols).

05

Pre-arrival preparation

Guide and staff briefing, itinerary walkthroughs, contingency scenario mapping. Emergency contact protocols and group leader communication established.

06

On-ground support

24/7 availability during group stay. Real-time coordination, incident response and guest satisfaction monitoring. Post-trip reporting and feedback collection.
